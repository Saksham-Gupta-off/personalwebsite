About Me

Hello! My name is Saksham Gupta.

I am currently a first year undergraduate student at the Department of Energy Sciences and Engineering, IITB (Mumbai, India).

Education

I have changed a lot of schools in my childhood due to transfers.

I completed my education from Nursery to 7th standard at Thakur Public School, Kandivali, Mumbai.

I then shifted to Panchkula and joined Bhawan Vidyalaya Chandigarh, and completed my class 10, obtaining 9.6 CGPA in CBSE Board Examinations in 2017.

I finished my junior college (11th and 12th standard) at Bhavan Vidyalaya Panchkula, with 96.4% in CBSE Board Examinations in 2019.
